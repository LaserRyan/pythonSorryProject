Objective of Game: Move all four of your pawns from the START to the HOME
How to play:
-insert names of players, 4 PLAYERS REQUIRED
-click to START
-follow the prompts given by the text int he center of the board
-might have to double click if computer didn't register!

Sorry Rules:
pawns can only be spawned when drawing a 1 or 2 *
pawns move by following the number of the drawn card
certain cards have special actions, follow the direction and also select which pawn(s) will be taking on that action
actions of each card is as follows:
1-start or move a pawn
2-start or move a pawn 2 steps, draw again regardless
3-move a pawn 3 steps
4- move a pawn back 4 steps
5-move a pawn 5 steps
7-split 7 across two pawns, cannot spawn a pawn this way
8-move a pawn 8 steps
10-move a pawn 10 steps or back 1 steps
11- move a pawn 11 steps or swap its location with a different colored pawn that isn't in their safetyzone
12-move a pawn 12 steps
sorry-if u still have unspawned pawns, pick the location of a different colored pawn that isn't in their safetyzone and bump them out, then take their spot
